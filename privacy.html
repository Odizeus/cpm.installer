Privacy Policy

This policy explains, in clear terms, how CPM Installer Services handles information shared through this website.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Information We Collect

When you request a quote or contact us, you may choose to provide information such as:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address.
  • The service you are interested in and the message or project details you send.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate and protect the website, such as information generated by your browser or hosting services.
  • Your language preference, which this site stores locally in your browser through localStorage.

How We Use Information

We use this information to respond to requests, prepare quotes, coordinate possible services, communicate about projects, and help keep the website secure and operating properly.

Forms and Service Providers

Quote requests are submitted through Netlify Forms. Our hosting and form-processing providers may process the information needed to deliver your message to CPM Installer Services and support related email notifications.

Sharing and Sale of Information

CPM Installer Services does not sell personal information. We may share information only with technical providers that help host the site, process forms, or deliver email notifications, and only as reasonably needed for those services.

Retention and Security

We keep quote requests for a reasonable period to respond, manage potential or completed work, and maintain ordinary business records.

We use reasonable measures intended to protect information, but no online system can promise absolute security.

Your Choices and Children

You may ask us to update or delete information you previously submitted by contacting us through the Contact page. This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly request their personal information.

Changes and Contact

We may update this policy as the site or our practices change. The date above shows the most recent revision. For privacy questions, please contact CPM Installer Services through the Contact page.
