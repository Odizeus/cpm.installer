About Us

Family-owned. Craft-focused. South Florida proud.

CPM Installer Services is a fully licensed and insured company located in Miami, serving all of South Florida with flooring installation and interior renewal.

Our Story

A reputation built one finished room at a time.

Established in October 2017 in the State of Florida, CPM Installer Services began as a family-owned business committed to straightforward communication and dependable craftsmanship.

For years, we have continued growing by maintaining high standards, completing projects on time and within budget, and treating every client space with care from project inception through completion.
